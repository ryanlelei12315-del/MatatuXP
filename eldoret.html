Why Eldoret

The North Rift Advantage

Eldoret is more than a transit hub — it's the heartbeat of the North Rift economy.

🏆

City of Champions

Eldoret is world-famous for Kenya's greatest distance runners. Many athletes use local matatus to reach training camps at Kaptagat and Iten — routes we track.

🏥

MTRH Corridor

The Moi Teaching & Referral Hospital is one of Kenya's largest. Dedicated matatu routes serve patients, staff and visitors along the hospital corridor daily.

🎓

University Routes

UoE and Moi University students rely on affordable matatus between campuses, town, and estates like Langas, Munyaka, Kapsoya, and Pioneer.
