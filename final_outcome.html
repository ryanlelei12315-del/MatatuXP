Why Eldoret

THE NORTH RIFT
ADVANTAGE

Eldoret is more than a transit hub — it's the heartbeat of the North Rift economy.

CITY OF CHAMPIONS

Eldoret is world-famous for Kenya's greatest distance runners. Athletes use local matatus to reach training camps at Kaptagat and Iten.

MTRH CORRIDOR

The Moi Teaching & Referral Hospital is one of Kenya's largest. Dedicated matatu routes serve patients, staff and visitors daily.

UNIVERSITY ROUTES

UoE and Moi University students rely on affordable matatus between campuses, town, and estates like Langas, Kapsoya, and Pioneer.
